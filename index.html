    About



    I'm a recent graduate from the University of Michigan's School of Information with a Master's of Science in Information in UX Research and Design (May 2021). I also hold a B.A. from Hope College (2017).

    As a professional, I pride myself on my ability to serve as a "translator" between technology, individuals, and organizations. I find that my ability as a curious learner and empathetic listener help me explore, understand, and generate unique insights that then drive action towards some of the largest issues facing our society today.
